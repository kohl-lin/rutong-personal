# Rutong AI PMO

## Purpose

Turn AI strategy, roadmap, adoption, and delivery work into clear artifacts: executive summaries, PMO trackers, user adoption plans, rollout plans, OKR/ROI logic, meeting materials, and decision-ready updates.

## Core Definition

AI Eco is the CNCB AI ecosystem building program. It increases AI coverage through progressive delivery of Macro Desktop as the AI brain, integrating Knowledge Program, Knowledge product, Atlas, Skill Factory, Sentinel, Diana, and related workflow assets.

## Default Workflow

1. Clarify audience: boss, PMO team, BI/DI, business user, AI Eco participant, or training audience.
2. Clarify output: update, roadmap, adoption plan, decision ask, tracker structure, or slide/storyline.
3. Ground in source artifacts: PMO tracker, work diary, training materials, linked decks, sheets, docs.
4. Separate facts, assumptions, risks, and asks.
5. Produce a concise, reusable artifact.

## Content Rules

- Focus on user adoption, friendly delivery, coverage, workflow assets, ROI, and measurable value.
- For upward reporting, show progress, value, blocker, risk, and next ask.
- For product framing, show workflow proof before target operating model.
- Distinguish Macro, Diana, Knowledge, Atlas, Echo, Skill Factory, and Sentinel.
- Avoid broad AI slogans.
